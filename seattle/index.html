🏔 Weekend Day Trips
🌋
Mt. Rainier National Park — Paradise Area
~2 hrs from Bellevue (94 mi via SR-167 S)  ·  Sat Apr 11 (recommended full day)
Must-Go Romantic Day Trip
Paradise Visitor Center, Skyline Trail (5 mi loop, moderate — stunning summit views), Reflection Lakes (mirror the mountain on calm days), Narada Falls (168 ft, 10-min stop). NPS entry: $35/car. Bring layers — snow in April!
💡 Leave by 7 AM for best parking and light. Pack a picnic.
💎
Diablo Lake — North Cascades NP
~3–3.5 hrs from Bellevue via SR-20  ·  Sat Apr 11 (alternative, early start)
Must-Go Day Trip Free Entry
Surreal turquoise glacier-fed lake. Diablo Lake Overlook pull-off, Washington Pass Overlook nearby. Longer drive but the color is unreal. Requires 5:30 AM departure for comfortable day.
⚠ Check SR-20 road opening at wsdot.wa.gov — may still be closed in early April.
🏘
Leavenworth — Bavarian Village in the Cascades
~2.5 hrs via US-2 E (115 mi)  ·  Apr 17 Fri eve + Apr 18 Sat morning
Must-Go Romantic ♡ Overnight
Charming Bavarian-themed town on the eastern slopes of the Cascades. Front Street walk, Icicle Creek Trail (1.3 mi easy loop on Blackbird Island), Icicle Ridge Winery, German food & wine bars. Magical at night when lit up.
♡ Most romantic spot on this trip. Highly recommend staying the night!
💦
Snoqualmie Falls
~30 min from Bellevue  ·  Easy add-on Sat Apr 18 on way back from Leavenworth
Quick Stop Romantic
268-ft waterfall in a stunning gorge. 5-min walk for upper viewpoint. 30-min hike to the base. Salish Lodge restaurant above the falls if you want a splurge brunch ($40/pp).
🌆 Seattle Evening Spots
🌄
Kerry Park — Iconic Skyline View
~25 min from Hyatt · W Highland Dr, Seattle · Free
Must-Go Romantic ♡ Free Evening
Best viewpoint in Seattle. Space Needle + Mt. Rainier behind the skyline on clear evenings. Go at sunset (~7:30–8 PM in April). Park on street.
🚀
Space Needle — Observation Deck
~25 min from Hyatt · Seattle Center · $35–40/pp
Must-Go Romantic ♡ Evening
360° views from 520 ft. Pair with Chihuly Garden of Glass next door (stunning art at night). Plan for Tue Apr 14 evening.
💡 Book online at spaceneedle.com for a sunset slot. Only worth it on clear evenings — check forecast!
🌿
Amazon Spheres
~25 min · 2111 7th Ave, Seattle · Free with employee guest pass
Must-Go Evening
Iconic glass domes filled with 40,000+ plants. Interior requires Amazon employee guest pass. Plan for Wed Apr 15 evening. Exterior is always free to photograph.
⚠ Contact Krishna dai for guest access — reach out before the trip!
⛵
Meydenbauer Bay Park — Sunset on the Water
~10 min walk from Hyatt · Bellevue · Free
Romantic ♡ Free Evening
Beautiful waterfront park on Lake Washington. Dock, sunset views, marina. Perfect for a quiet evening walk — just 5 min from the Hyatt. A local gem ♡
⛽
Gas Works Park + Lake Union
~25 min · Seattle · Free
Romantic Free Evening
Industrial-turned-park on Lake Union with panoramic Seattle skyline views. Seattle's most unique park. Great for the Thu Apr 16 evening.
